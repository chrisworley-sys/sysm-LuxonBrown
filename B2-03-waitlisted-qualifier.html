Hi [First Name],

The fact that you signed up for our seminar — even if you weren't able to get a seat — tells us you're thinking seriously about your financial future. That matters, and we want to make sure you're getting the support you need.

One of the most important things we've learned from working with hundreds of families is this: a financial plan is only as good as its ability to adapt to your life. And life has a way of changing when you least expect it.

We'd like to ask — has anything shifted for you recently? Some of the most common life changes that signal it's time to revisit your plan include:

  • Getting married or entering a domestic partnership
  • Approaching retirement or receiving an early retirement offer
  • Losing a spouse or going through a divorce
  • Receiving an inheritance or a significant financial windfall
  • A major change in income, employment, or business ownership

If any of these apply to you — or if something else has changed — we'd love to hear about it. Our short planning questionnaire takes just a few minutes and helps us understand your situation before we ever have a conversation.
