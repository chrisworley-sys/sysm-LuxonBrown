What You'll Learn

Five Decisions That Shape Your Retirement

This presentation covers the planning decisions that have the greatest impact on your financial security — and the mistakes most families make before it's too late to correct them.

01

When to Claim Social Security

The timing of your Social Security claim can mean a difference of $100,000 or more over your lifetime. We'll show you how to think through this decision the right way.

02

How to Create Reliable Income

Turning a retirement account into a paycheck requires a strategy. We'll walk through the approaches that help ensure your money lasts as long as you do.

03

Managing Taxes in Retirement

Many retirees are surprised by how much they owe in taxes. We'll explain how the right withdrawal strategy can significantly reduce your tax burden over time.

04

Planning for Healthcare Costs

Healthcare is one of the largest — and most unpredictable — expenses in retirement. We'll cover what you need to know about Medicare, long-term care, and coverage gaps.

05

Protecting Your Legacy

Your estate plan determines what happens to everything you've built. We'll cover the key documents every family needs and the common mistakes that leave loved ones unprotected.
