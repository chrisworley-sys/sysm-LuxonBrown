The Process
6-Step Streamlined Workflow

From content calendar planning through publication — with a built-in compliance decision gate that routes content through the most efficient review path.

1

Content Calendar Planning

3–5 Days SuperSymm

Quarterly calendar with consolidated campaigns. Similar demographics and goals grouped together to reduce total content volume for review.

2

Content Creation

3–5 Days SuperSymm

Emails, ads, and social posts developed. Content batched for bi-weekly review cycle — all content types on the same timeline.

3

Internal Review

48 Hours Luxon Brown — Taylor / Chris M

Content submitted to SuperSymm Compliance Hub. Team notified for review with options to Approve, Edit, provide Feedback, or Flag for External Review.

4

Compliance Decision Point

24 Hours Chris M — Compliance Officer

Does content need external verification? Contains numbers or statistics? Any regulatory uncertainty?

20–30%

→ External Review (Comply)

Flagged content submitted for external compliance review. 2–7 day turnaround with feedback loop if revisions needed.

70–80%

→ Internal Approval

Content approved by internal review and moves directly to publication queue. No external review required.

5

Revisions & Final Approval

1–2 Days SuperSymm

Any feedback from internal or external review is incorporated. Final versions confirmed and queued for publication.

6

Content Publication

Per Schedule SuperSymm / Luxon Brown

Approved content scheduled per content calendar. Emails sent, social posts published, ads launched. Performance tracking begins.

Two Tracks, Clear Deadlines

Why It Works
Key Benefits

A process designed to be faster, more predictable, and scalable — without sacrificing compliance rigor.

⚡

Faster Turnaround

2-week fast track for internally-approved content. Parallel workflows allow multiple batches in progress simultaneously.

🛡️

Comprehensive Coverage

Two-tier review system with clear ownership at each stage. Compliance officer oversight ensures regulatory requirements are met.

📊

Manageable Volume

Content consolidation reduces total pieces. Bi-weekly batching prevents reviewer overwhelm with only 20–30% requiring Comply.

🎯

Clear Expectations

Defined timelines for each stage — 48 hrs internal, 2–7 days external. Predictable 2–3 week lead time for all stakeholders.

💰

Cost Efficient

Reduced external review volume lowers compliance costs. Internal review catches most issues before costly external submissions.

🔄

Scalable Framework

Quarterly planning with bi-weekly execution. Consistent content flow maintained as marketing volume increases over time.
